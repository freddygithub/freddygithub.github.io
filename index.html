League of Legends most successful and fun bot lane combinations

Mord and Pyke

Pyke's pull allows for Mordekaiser to get up close and maximize Q damage

Ziggs and Brand

Lane clear that pushes any adc out of lane, with high burst for early game win

Yasuo and Nami

Bubble and tidal wave allow for Yasuo to get close and do maximum damage

Swain and Blitz

Swains demon eye push's enemies into a blitz pull, while nevermore allows for free blitz knockup

Miss Fortune and Poppy

Push the enimey adc into a mf ulti or push them so mf can prock her Q

Jihn and Vel'Koz

Long range poke that deals heavy damage, vel'koz should knock up on jihns traps

Zilean and Karma

Karma's stun allows for Zilean to get a free stun, and both have a speed boost for ganks and dodging

Morg and Zyra

Morg can stun with q or ulti to get zyra set up for a combo

Lucian and Braum

Classic lane with Lucians pasive comboing nicely with Braums pasive

Draven and Darius

Heavy damage early game that relies on bully play style

Ezreal and Taric

Taric can go deap in for the stuns while relying on ez to close the distance and still come out alive

Vayne and Nunu

A Nunu slow and buff allows for an easy lane with vayne

Hover over the pictures to get a short description on gameplay tactics. Feel free to contact us with your bot lane meta choice and tell us how it works!
