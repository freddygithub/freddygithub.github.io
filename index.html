League of Legends most successful and fun bot lane combinations

Mord and Pyke

Pyke's pull allows for Mordekaiser to get up close and maximize Q damage

Ziggs and Brand

Lane clear that pushes any adc out of lane, with high burst for early game win

Yasuo and Nami

Bubble and tidal wave allow for Yasuo to get close and do maximum damage

Swain and Blitz

Swains demon eye push's enemies into a blitz pull, while nevermore allows for free blitz knockup

Miss Fortune and Poppy

Push the enimey adc into a mf ulti or push them so mf can prock her Q

Jihn and Vel'Koz

Long range poke that deals heavy damage, vel'koz should knock up on jihns traps

Zilean and Karma

Karma's stun allows for Zilean to get a free stun, and both have a speed boost for ganks and dodging

Morg and Zyra

Morg can stun with q or ulti to get zyra set up for a combo

Lucian and Braum

Classic lane with Lucians pasive comboing nicely with Braums pasive

Draven and Darius

Heavy damage early game that relies on bully play style

Ezreal and Taric

Taric can go deap in for the stuns while relying on ez to close the distance and still come out alive

Vayne and Nunu

A Nunu slow and buff allows for an easy lane with vayne


Guides

Mordekaiser Bot guide:

You can start with relic shield, if you have a support that is dmg that needs gold this is good, or you can start with dorans shield to have a little more sustain when laning. You will need to play passive until you have your E and Q. After this you can out damage most ADC’s IF you get the last proc of the Q off. That requires a support with either a long stun or a pull, and preferably both. Once you hit level 6, your top priority should be dragon. Your ulti allows you to control a ghost dragon. This should be used along with the jungle to get that first tower gold bottom lane. Past 6 just stay on dragon since it extremely benefits your team over the others and remember to ulti the enemy ADC in team fights. With the enemy ADC you can easily tower shred or baron quickly.

Ziggs Bot guide:

Start with Dorans ring. You don’t want to fight until you have poked the ADC down with you Q. You will mostly lose all trades when everyone is full health. Make sure to farm and push then, that is what Ziggs is made for. Once you get your ulti, you should use it preemptively or to aid mid. Remember your satchel charge can execute towers and be very powerful at escaping hard situations.

Nami Bot guide:

You want to start with Spellthief’s edge and be relentless with your W. Time your bubbles to hit just slightly behind your enemies. Take note that your E can grant you assists when buffing allies and cost very little mana. Use your ulti just after enemies lands from your bubble. You want to get good at poking an escaping, you should almost never fully commit to a fight. You are useful for poke and bounce healing. If an ally is close enough to the enemies you poke, your W will bounce off and heal them.
